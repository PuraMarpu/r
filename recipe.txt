ingredients:
A bowl of water;
Maggi pack;
some corriander;
onions;
chillies;

direction:
boil some water in the bowl.While boiling it dice some coriander, chillies and green onions. After the water is boiled unpack the maggi pack and add it; you might crush it if you like to make the noodle strips smaller. Stir the maggi slowly while adding the onions and chillies. After it is properly cooked add the corriander as a topping.
